1. Introduction

IASE (hereinafter referred to as "we", "us", or "our") respects and protects the privacy of all users of our services (hereinafter referred to as "you" or "your"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal information.

Please read and understand this Privacy Policy carefully before using our services.

2. Information Collection

We may collect the following types of information:

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you log in via Facebook, we may access your Facebook public profile (nickname, profile picture, etc.)
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number, WhatsApp number, etc.
  • Course Information: Surfing course bookings, learning progress, evaluation feedback, etc.
  • Communication Records: Your communications with our partners or customer service team

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, browser type, etc.
  • Log Information: Access time, IP address, page browsing records, etc.
  • Location Information: Geolocation information with your authorization (used to recommend nearby surf spots)

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  1. Provide, maintain, and improve our surfing course services
  2. Process your course bookings and payments
  3. Communicate with you about course arrangements, changes, or updates
  4. Send service-related notifications (such as course reminders, evaluation invitations)
  5. Provide customer support and technical assistance
  6. Analyze usage to optimize user experience
  7. Prevent fraud and ensure service security

4. Information Sharing

We will not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

4.1 Sharing with Service Providers

To provide services, we may share necessary student information with partners (such as surf clubs, coaches), including:

  • Name and contact information (for course arrangements)
  • Course type and schedule
  • Emergency contact information (for safety purposes)

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or to protect our legitimate rights and interests.

4.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset transfer, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will notify you in advance.

5. Information Protection

We take reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your information security:

  • Use encryption technology to transmit sensitive data

  • Conduct regular security audits and risk assessments

However, please note that internet transmission cannot be guaranteed 100% secure, and we cannot absolutely guarantee information security.

Under applicable laws, you may have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: Request access to your personal information we hold

  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal information under certain circumstances
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent to information processing at any time

To exercise the above rights, please contact us using the contact information below.
