Data Deletion Policy

Last Updated: March 24, 2026
📋 Your Rights: You have the right to request deletion of your personal data at any time. We will process your request in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

1. Overview

IASE respects your right to privacy and data control. This Data Deletion Policy explains how you can request the deletion of your personal data from our systems and how we will process such requests.

We comply with Facebook's Platform Policy and applicable data protection laws, including GDPR and CCPA.

2. What Data Can Be Deleted

You may request deletion of the following types of data:

  • Account Information: Facebook profile data, user ID, login credentials
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number, WhatsApp number
  • Course Records: Booking history, learning progress, evaluation records
  • Communication Records: Messages with customer service or partners
  • Device Information: Logs, IP addresses, browser information
  • Other Personal Data: Any other personally identifiable information we hold about you

3. Data Retention Exceptions

Certain data may be retained even after a deletion request due to:

  • Legal Requirements: Tax laws, financial regulations, or other legal obligations
  • Ongoing Services: Active course bookings that have not been completed
  • Dispute Resolution: Data needed for ongoing legal proceedings or disputes
  • Security Purposes: Data required to prevent fraud or protect user safety

Such data will be deleted as soon as the retention requirement no longer applies.

4. How to Request Data Deletion

1Method 1: Email Request (Recommended)

Send a deletion request to:

Email: office@surfplus.club

Subject Line: Data Deletion Request

Include in your email:

  • Your full name
  • Email address associated with your account
  • Facebook user ID (if known)
  • Brief reason for deletion (optional)

2Method 2: WhatsApp Request

Send a message to our customer service:

WhatsApp: +66 81 052 0715

Message Template:

"I request deletion of all my personal data from IASE systems. My account email is: [your email]"

3Method 3: Facebook App (If Available)

If our Facebook app provides an in-app deletion feature:

  1. Open the app
  2. Go to Settings → Privacy
  3. Click "Delete My Data"
  4. Confirm your request

5. Processing Timeline

After receiving your request:

  • Acknowledgment: We will confirm receipt within 3 business days
  • Verification: We may contact you to verify your identity (within 5 business days)
  • Processing: Data deletion will be completed within 30 days
  • Completion Notice: You will receive confirmation once deletion is complete

6. What Happens After Deletion

After your data is deleted:

  • Your account will be permanently closed
  • You will no longer be able to access course history or bookings
  • All personal identifiers will be removed from our systems
  • Anonymous analytics data may be retained (not linked to your identity)

Note: Data deletion is irreversible. If you wish to use our services again, you will need to create a new account.

7. Facebook-Specific Data

For data shared via Facebook Login:

  • We will delete all data obtained through Facebook API
  • We will notify Facebook of the data deletion (as required by Platform Policy)
  • You may also revoke app permissions in your Facebook settings

To revoke app permissions on Facebook:

  1. Go to Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites
  2. Find "IASE" in your active apps
  3. Click "Remove"
  4. Check "Delete all posts, photos and videos"
  5. Click "Remove" to confirm

8. Children's Data

If we discover that we have collected data from a child under 18 without parental consent, we will delete it immediately. Parents can request deletion of their child's data by contacting us with proof of guardianship.

9. Appeals

If you are not satisfied with how we handled your deletion request, you may:

  • Contact us for reconsideration
  • File a complaint with your local data protection authority
  • Seek legal remedies as provided by applicable law
